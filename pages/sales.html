Sales Insights Project

Problem Statement

AtliQ Hardware is a company that supplies computer hardware and peripherals to their clients with it head office in New Dehli India and has regional offices around the country. Bhavya Patel, the sales director of the company is having a challenge tracking sales in a dynamically growing market. The regional managers contact Bhavya Patel over a phone call to give him insights of sales and the problem with this is that it is verbal communication and the managers have this habit of trying to sugar coat the facts or painting a rosy picture because they don’t want to look bad.

Bhavya Patel is getting frustrated because he can notice sales are declining but when talking to his managers, he not getting the complete picture. When he asks for numbers, they give him a lot of Excel files whereas he just needs to understand in simplistic terms how is the business performing, what are the weakest areas they need to focus on and if there is a decline in sales in a certain region maybe they can look to start some promotion offers or engage with customers in a better way. He is interested in getting a simple and digestible insights

Project Planining Using Aims Grid

Purpose;
• To unlock sales insights that were not visible before to sales team for decision support
• To automate these insights to reduce manual time spent in data gathering

Stakeholders;
• Sales Director
• Marketing team
• Customer Service team
• Data Analytics team
• IT team

End Result;
An automated dashboard providing quick and latest sales insights in order to support data driven decision making

Success Criteria;
• Dashboard uncovering sales orders insights with latest data available
• Sales team to be able to take better decisions and improve cost savings
• Sales Analyst to stop data gathering manually in order to save 20% of their business time and revest it elsewhere
• Improvement in sales roughly after 3 months

Summary

The dashboard was developed in Power BI and before it was built the first step into solving this problem was accessing the database where all the sales data was stored and then running some queries to get some quick overview and understanding the data. The second step involved the ETL process which is Extract Transform and Load where the data was extracted from MySQL database to Power Query in Power BI where it was cleaned.
